Trading Timeframe

The "Pulse" of the Bot

Controls how fast the bot thinks. 5 Minute is for Day Trading (faster). 30 Minute is for Swing Trading (slower, more reliable).

Risk per Trade ($)

The "Bet Size"

This is NOT how much you buy. It is how much you are willing to lose if a trade fails. The bot calculates your share size automatically based on this number.

Live Strategies (Checkboxes)

The "Playbook"

You can check multiple boxes. The bot will scan for ALL of them at once. If "Trend Pullback" and "Momentum Cross" are both checked, it looks for both.

ATR Stop Multiplier

The "Safety Net"

This sets your Stop Loss. A higher number (e.g., 2.0) gives the stock more room to wiggle. A lower number (e.g., 1.0) cuts losses very quickly.

ATR Profit Multiplier

The "Goal Line"

This sets where you sell for a win. Usually, you want this number to be 2x your Stop Multiplier (e.g., Risk 1.0, Profit 2.0).

Trade Mode

Day vs. Swing

Day Trade: Sells everything before the market closes (Stocks only).
Swing Trade: Holds stocks overnight or for days.

Included Strategies

Stock Strategies

  • Trend Pullback (BB+Stoch) Buys when an uptrending stock dips slightly (a "discount") and shows signs of bouncing back up.
  • Pattern + Confirm Strict. Finds bullish candlestick patterns (Hammer, Engulfing) confirmed by MACD and RSI indicators.
  • Momentum Cross Chases momentum. Buys when the MACD lines cross upward while the Stochastic indicator is also rising.

Crypto Strategies

  • Trend Follow (EMA 50/200) The "Golden Cross." Buys when the short-term trend crosses above the long-term trend.
  • Breakout (BB+Vol) Buys when price explodes above the Bollinger Bands with a massive spike in volume. High risk, high reward.
  • Mean Reversion Buys when a coin is "Oversold" (RSI < 30) and the price is statistically too low (Low Bollinger Band).
