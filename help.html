Getting Started with TradeBotPro

Follow these simple steps to set up your Alpaca trading account and connect it to TradeBotPro.

1

Access the Alpaca Trading Platform

TradeBotPro uses the Alpaca API for seamless and commission-free trading. First, navigate to the official Alpaca Markets website.

Go to Alpaca.Markets ↗
2

Create and Verify Your Alpaca Account

On the Alpaca website, click on "Sign Up" or "Get Started" to begin the account creation process. You will need to provide personal information, link your bank account, and complete identity verification (KYC). This process is standard for brokerage accounts.

  • Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started".
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to enter your details.
  • Complete identity verification (KYC).
  • Fund your account.
3

Generate Your Alpaca API Keys

Once your Alpaca account is set up and verified, you'll need to generate your API keys. These keys allow TradeBotPro to securely communicate with your Alpaca account.

  1. Log in to your Alpaca Dashboard.
  2. Navigate to the "API Keys" section (usually found under "Account" or "Settings").
  3. Click on "Generate New Key" or similar.
  4. You will be provided with an **API Key ID** and a **Secret Key**.
  5. IMPORTANT: Copy both keys immediately! The Secret Key is usually shown only once. If you lose it, you'll have to regenerate it.
4

Input API Keys into TradeBotPro

Open your downloaded TradeBotPro application. On the main interface, you will find fields for "API Key" and "Secret Key".

  • Paste your **API Key ID** into the "API Key" field.
  • Paste your **Secret Key** into the "Secret Key" field.
  • Click the "Start Bot" button.

Ensure you are using the correct keys for your Paper Trading (testing) or Live Trading account.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is TradeBotPro suitable for beginners? ▼

TradeBotPro is designed to automate trading, which can be beneficial for beginners by handling complex decisions. However, understanding basic trading concepts and risks is highly recommended. We advise starting with a paper trading account on Alpaca.

What kind of returns can I expect? ▼

We cannot guarantee any specific returns. Trading performance is subject to market volatility, economic conditions, and the specific strategies employed. Past performance is not indicative of future results. It's crucial to understand that trading involves significant risk, and losses can exceed initial investments.

Do I need to keep my computer running 24/7? ▼

For continuous operation during market hours and for the bot to automatically resume, your computer needs to be running. Many users opt for a Virtual Private Server (VPS) to ensure uninterrupted operation without needing their personal computer on.

What is fractional share trading? ▼

Fractional share trading allows you to buy a portion of a stock, rather than a full share. This means you can invest smaller amounts of money into high-priced stocks, diversifying your portfolio more easily and utilizing all your capital.

Is options trading included in the Lite version? ▼

No, the experimental options trading module is exclusively available in the TradeBotPro Pro Version. The Lite version is limited to traditional stock (equity) trading.
