Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. TradeBotPro is a downloadable Windows application. You install it like any other program, enter your Alpaca API keys, and click Start. Zero coding required at any step.

What is Alpaca? Do I need an account?

Alpaca is a free, commission-free brokerage API. You sign up at alpaca.markets, complete identity verification, generate your API keys, and paste them into TradeBotPro. You can start in Paper Trading (fake money) mode for free with no deposit required.

Is my money at risk?

Paper Trading mode uses completely fake money — nothing is at risk, no deposit required. The bot will only trade with real money if you explicitly select "Live Trading" in Settings and confirm the warning prompt. We strongly recommend starting in Paper Trading to learn the bot's behavior first.

What if the bot makes bad trades?

You set a Daily Stop-Loss Limit in Settings. If the bot loses more than that amount in a single day, it automatically stops placing new trades. Every trade also has an individual stop-loss based on ATR, so no single trade can blow up your account. The "Force Sell All" button on the dashboard lets you exit everything instantly.

Does it work on Mac?

Currently Windows 10 and Windows 11 only. Mac support is on our roadmap. If you need 24/7 operation without keeping your PC on, consider running the bot on a Windows VPS (Virtual Private Server).

Do I need to keep my computer on?

Yes — the bot runs on your local machine. If you close it, it stops. Your open positions and P/L are saved to a local database, so nothing is lost when you restart. For 24/7 crypto trading, a Windows VPS running the bot 24/7 is the best solution.

What is the Trailing Stop and why does it matter?

A trailing stop automatically moves your stop-loss upward as a trade becomes profitable. For example: if a stock rises 1%, your stop moves to breakeven (you can't lose). As it keeps rising, the stop trails below the highest price — locking in more profit without cutting your winner short. It's one of the most powerful risk tools available to retail traders.
